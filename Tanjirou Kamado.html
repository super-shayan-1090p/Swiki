About Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonist of Koyoharu Gotouge's Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He was once a kind-hearted charcoal seller living peacefully with his family in the mountains until his family was slaughtered by demons, with only his sister Nezuko surviving the attack - though she was turned into a demon.

"No matter how many people you may lose, you have no choice but to go on living. No matter how devastating the blows may be."

Determined to find a cure for his sister and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to battle demons and protect humanity while never losing his compassionate nature.

Personality & Character

Tanjiro is characterized by his exceptional kindness, strong sense of justice, and unwavering determination. Despite the tragedy he's endured, he maintains a pure heart and shows compassion even toward demons.

Compassionate Determined Empathetic Persevering Protective Strong-willed

Tanjiro's most remarkable quality is his ability to sense the emotions and memories of others through his enhanced sense of smell, which allows him to understand even his enemies' pain and suffering.

Abilities & Powers

Tanjiro possesses unique abilities that make him one of the most formidable Demon Slayers in history.

Enhanced Sense of Smell

Can detect emotions, track demons, identify weaknesses, and even sense the "Opening Thread" - lines that show where to strike for maximum effect.

Water Breathing

Mastered all 10 forms of Water Breathing under Urokodaki's training, creating fluid, powerful attacks that mimic water's movements.

Hinokami Kagura

Inherited the Sun Breathing technique from his father, the original and most powerful Breathing Style that can seriously harm Muzan Kibutsuji.

Demon Slayer Mark

Awakened during intense battles, enhancing his physical abilities, speed, and reaction time to superhuman levels.

Transparent World

Can see into his opponents' bodies, observing muscle movements, blood flow, and joint actions to predict attacks.

Bright Red Nichirin Sword

His blade turns bright red when using Hinokami Kagura, temporarily neutralizing demon regeneration abilities.

The Kamado Legacy

Tanjiro comes from a long line of practitioners of the Hinokami Kagura (Dance of the Fire God), which was actually the original Sun Breathing technique passed down through generations to keep it from being lost.

Unique Perspective

What sets Tanjiro apart is his compassion toward demons. While dedicated to eliminating demons that harm humans, he recognizes their tragic origins and often prays for them as they die, acknowledging their suffering.

Demon Slayer Rank

Tanjiro quickly rises through the Demon Slayer Corps ranks, eventually becoming a Hashira-level combatant recognized for his exceptional skills and contributions to defeating the Twelve Kizuki and Muzan himself.
