About Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi Fushiguro is one of the main protagonists of Gege Akutami's Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a first-year jujutsu sorcerer at Tokyo Jujutsu High and a member of the prestigious Zenin clan, though he rejects his family name. Megumi specializes in the Ten Shadows Technique, one of the most powerful inherited techniques in the jujutsu world.

"I don't need to know if I'm a cog in the machine. I'll keep moving forward until I die."

Despite his serious and pragmatic demeanor, Megumi possesses a strong moral compass and will risk everything to save people he believes are worth saving. His growth throughout the series showcases his incredible potential as a jujutsu sorcerer.

Personality & Character

Megumi appears cold, logical, and emotionally reserved, but this masks a deeply caring individual who values human life above all else. He operates on a utilitarian philosophy but is willing to break his own rules for those he deems worth saving.

Serious Pragmatic Loyal Analytical Protective Morally Complex

Megumi's character development involves learning to value his own life and potential while balancing his pragmatic approach to jujutsu with his inherent desire to protect innocent people.

Abilities & Powers

As a prodigy from the Zenin clan, Megumi possesses exceptional jujutsu skills and one of the most versatile techniques in the jujutsu world.

Ten Shadows Technique

Can summon and control ten different shikigami through shadows. Each shikigami has unique abilities and can be combined for complex strategies.

Domain Expansion: Chimera Shadow Garden

His incomplete Domain creates a garden of shadows where he can spawn unlimited shikigami and use shadows more freely.

Shadow Storage

Can store objects and weapons within shadows, allowing him to retrieve them instantly during combat.

Strategic Combat

Excellent analytical skills and tactical thinking. Can assess opponents' abilities quickly and devise effective counterstrategies.

Cursed Energy Control

Proficient in cursed energy manipulation with precise control, allowing for efficient use of his techniques.

Hand-to-Hand Combat

Skilled martial artist trained in close-quarters combat, capable of holding his own even without shikigami.

The Ten Shadows Legacy

The Ten Shadows Technique is one of the two great techniques of the Zenin Clan, rivaled only by the Gojo Clan's Six Eyes. Historically, users of these techniques often killed each other in battle.

Moral Philosophy

Megumi operates on a unique moral code: he won't save everyone, but he will save those he judges as "worth saving." This pragmatic approach often puts him at odds with Yuji's more idealistic worldview.

Domain Expansion

Megumi's Chimera Shadow Garden is unique among Domains - it's incomplete but doesn't have a sure-hit effect, instead enhancing his shadows and allowing free shikigami creation.

Growth & Potential

Throughout the series, Megumi evolves from a competent but limited sorcerer to someone recognized as having the potential to reach Special Grade level, with Gojo noting he could eventually rival even him.
