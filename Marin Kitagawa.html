The Radiant Cosplay Enthusiast

About Marin Kitagawa

Marin Kitagawa is the main female protagonist of Shinichi Fukuda's My Dress-Up Darling (Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo Suru). She's a popular, outgoing high school student who becomes fascinated with cosplay after meeting Wakana Gojo, a skilled hina doll artisan.

"KYAAA! This is so amazing! You're seriously incredible, Gojo-kun!"

With her bright personality and infectious enthusiasm, Marin brings joy and energy to everyone around her. She's unapologetically passionate about her hobbies, from cosplay to video games and anime, and isn't afraid to show her true self to the world.

Personality & Character

Marin is characterized by her boundless energy, genuine enthusiasm, and lack of social filters. She's incredibly expressive and wears her heart on her sleeve, making her both endearing and unforgettable to those around her.

Energetic Expressive Passionate Supportive Confident Genuine

Despite being popular and attractive, Marin remains down-to-earth and values genuine connections over superficial relationships. Her friendship with Wakana develops into a deep bond built on mutual respect and shared passions.

The "KYAAA!" Energy

Marin is famous for her expressive reactions, particularly her excited "KYAAA!" exclamations when she's thrilled or impressed. This genuine enthusiasm becomes infectious and helps break down Gojo's social barriers.

Cosplay Philosophy

For Marin, cosplay isn't just about wearing costumes - it's about becoming the character she loves and sharing that passion with others. She sees it as the ultimate form of appreciation for her favorite series.

Fashion Sense

Marin has a distinctive fashion style featuring gyaru-inspired elements mixed with cute, feminine touches. Her signature look includes her blonde hair with pink tips and trendy, expressive outfits.

Growth & Development

While Marin starts as the confident one teaching Gojo about cosplay culture, she also grows significantly through their relationship, learning about craftsmanship, dedication, and the beauty of traditional arts.
