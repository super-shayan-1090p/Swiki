SHIGEO KAGEYAMA

The Powerful Esper Known as "Mob"

About Shigeo Kageyama

Shigeo Kageyama, nicknamed "Mob" (meaning "background character"), is the main protagonist of the manga and anime series Mob Psycho 100, created by ONE. Despite possessing immense psychic powers, Mob strives to live a normal life and not rely on his abilities.

"My psychic powers aren't what make me who I am. I'm Shigeo Kageyama. I'm Mob."

Mob works as an assistant to the self-proclaimed psychic Arataka Reigen, helping clients with supernatural problems. He attends Salt Middle School where he tries to fit in and live an ordinary student life.

Personality & Character Traits

Mob is typically quiet, reserved, and emotionally suppressed. He consciously limits his emotional expression to prevent his psychic powers from going out of control.

Humble Empathetic Determined Selfless Emotionally Repressed Hardworking

Mob's emotional state is represented by a percentage meter that fills as he experiences strong emotions. When it reaches 100%, his powers erupt uncontrollably.

Psychic Abilities

Mob possesses some of the most powerful psychic abilities in his universe, though he rarely uses them to their full potential by choice.

Telekinesis

Can move objects with his mind, from small items to entire buildings.

Energy Manipulation

Can create barriers, energy blasts, and psychic constructs.

Astral Projection

Can separate his consciousness from his physical body.

Exorcism

Can purify or destroy evil spirits and psychic entities.

Philosophy

Mob believes that psychic powers don't make someone special and that everyone has their own strengths. He values self-improvement through effort rather than relying on his natural abilities.
