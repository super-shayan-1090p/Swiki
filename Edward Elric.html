About Edward Elric

Edward Elric, known as the Fullmetal Alchemist, is the protagonist of Hiromu Arakawa's Fullmetal Alchemist series. He and his younger brother Alphonse committed the ultimate alchemical taboo - human transmutation - in an attempt to resurrect their deceased mother.

"A lesson without pain is meaningless. That's because you can't gain anything without sacrificing something else in return."

As punishment for their transgression, Edward lost his left leg and Alphonse lost his entire body. Edward sacrificed his right arm to bind Alphonse's soul to a suit of armor. Now, as a State Alchemist, Edward searches for the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies.

Personality & Character

Edward is fiercely determined, intelligent, and protective of those he cares about, particularly his brother Alphonse. Despite his short temper and prideful nature, he possesses a strong moral compass and sense of justice.

Determined Brilliant Protective Hot-tempered Compassionate Resourceful

Edward is extremely sensitive about his height and reacts violently to being called "short" or any related terms, though he grows significantly throughout the series.

The Gate of Truth

Edward saw the Truth beyond the Gate when he attempted human transmutation, gaining immense alchemical knowledge but paying the toll of his limbs. The Gate contains all alchemical knowledge of the universe.

Automail

Edward's right arm and left leg are advanced prosthetic limbs created and maintained by Winry Rockbell. The automail provides enhanced strength but requires regular maintenance and causes significant pain.

State Alchemist

As a State Alchemist, Edward holds the military rank of Major and is known by the code name "Fullmetal" due to his automail limbs. The position gives him access to resources for his research but also makes him a "dog of the military."
