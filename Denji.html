About Denji

Denji is the protagonist of Tatsuki Fujimoto's manga series Chainsaw Man. He starts as a poverty-stricken young man forced to repay his father's debt by hunting devils with his pet devil-dog Pochita.

"I wanna eat toast with jam... I wanna touch some boobs... I want a girlfriend!"

After being betrayed and killed, Denji merges with Pochita and becomes the Chainsaw Man - a human-devil hybrid with the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws.

Personality & Character

Denji begins as a simple-minded youth with extremely low expectations for life, having known nothing but poverty and exploitation. His desires are basic: food, shelter, and human connection.

Simple-Minded Loyal Brutally Honest Emotionally Scarred Surprisingly Clever Yearning for Normalcy

Despite his rough exterior and simplistic desires, Denji possesses a strong moral compass and grows emotionally throughout the series, though his development is often messy and complicated.

Abilities & Powers

As the Chainsaw Devil hybrid, Denji possesses incredible regenerative abilities and combat prowess.

Chainsaw Transformation

Can manifest chainsaws from his head, arms, and legs by pulling a cord on his chest.

Devil Hybrid Physiology

Enhanced strength, speed, and near-instant regeneration from blood consumption.

Blood Consumption

Drinking blood accelerates his healing factor and powers his transformations.

Devil Erasure

Chainsaw Man can permanently erase devils by consuming them, removing their concept from existence.

Key Relationships

  • Pochita - The Chainsaw Devil, his partner and heart
  • Makima - The Control Devil, his manipulator
  • Power - The Blood Devil, his "little sister"
  • Aki Hayakawa - His Public Safety partner
  • Kobeni - Fellow Public Safety devil hunter

Notable Forms

  • Human Form: Normal appearance
  • Hybrid Form: Partial chainsaw transformation
  • Chainsaw Man: Full devil transformation
  • Hero of Hell: True Chainsaw Devil form

Public Safety Division

Denji works for Tokyo's Special Division 4 of Public Safety, hunting devils under Makima's command in exchange for basic living necessities and the chance at a normal life.

Iconic Features

Denji's most recognizable traits include his chainsaw cord pull-starter on his chest and his devilish grin when transformed.
