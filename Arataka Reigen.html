Character Overview

Arataka Reigen is one of the most unique and beloved characters from ONE's manga and anime series "Mob Psycho 100." He begins as a fraudulent psychic running a spiritual consultation business but evolves into a genuine mentor and father figure.

The brilliant twist: Reigen has absolutely no psychic powers whatsoever, yet he's one of the most effective "exorcists" in the series.

  • Master of Psychology
    The Real Power Was Emotional Intelligence All Along

    Reigen's true strength lies in his understanding of human psychology. He can read people's emotions, fears, and motivations with uncanny accuracy, allowing him to provide genuine help despite his fraudulent premise.

  • Exceptional Oratory Skills

    Reigen can talk his way out of (and into) almost any situation. His speeches are so compelling that he can convince powerful espers, evil spirits, and entire organizations.

  • Crisis Management

    He remains calm and analytical in dangerous situations, quickly assessing threats and formulating plans despite having no actual powers to protect himself.

  • Business Acumen

    From marketing to client relations to financial management, Reigen demonstrates legitimate business skills that keep his consultation office running successfully.

Why Reigen is Iconic

The Anti-Hero Mentor

A flawed, fraudulent character who becomes one of anime's most genuine and effective mentors through sheer force of personality and wisdom.

Subversion of Expectations

In a world of superpowered espers, the most effective character has no powers at all, proving that emotional intelligence and people skills are true superpowers.

Character Growth

His journey from selfish conman to selfless mentor is one of the most satisfying character arcs in modern anime.

Comic Relief with Depth

While hilarious in his antics, Reigen's wisdom and genuine care for Mob give him surprising emotional weight.

"Listen up, Mob. It's okay to run away. It's okay to not have a dream. You can just be a normal, kind person."

In short, Arataka Reigen is the charismatic, salt-throwing, self-proclaimed greatest psychic of the 21st century who, despite having no actual powers, becomes one of the most effective and beloved mentors in anime through sheer force of personality and genuine wisdom.
