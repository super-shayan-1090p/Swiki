About Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is one of the most prominent characters in the Naruto series, known as "Kakashi of the Sharingan" and famously called "Copy Ninja Kakashi." He is a Jonin of Konohagakure and the leader of Team 7.

"Those who break the rules are scum, but those who abandon their friends are worse than scum."

Throughout the series, Kakashi serves as a mentor to Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno, eventually becoming the Sixth Hokage.

Personality & Character Traits

Kakashi presents himself as aloof and detached, often arriving late to appointments and reading his favorite book series, Make-Out Paradise. However, beneath this exterior lies a deeply caring individual dedicated to his students and village.

Kakashi carries the trauma of losing his father and teammates, which shaped his philosophy about protecting comrades at all costs.

Ninja Abilities & Jutsu

Kakashi is one of the most skilled shinobi in the Naruto universe, known for his versatility and intelligence in battle.

Sharingan

Gifted by Obito Uchiha, allowing him to copy over 1,000 jutsu.

Chidori

His signature lightning-style technique, requiring Sharingan to use safely.

Lightning Blade

An enhanced version of Chidori that became his trademark technique.

Kamui

A space-time ninjutsu that allows him to warp objects to another dimension.

Summoning Technique

Can summon ninja dogs, particularly his ninken pack led by Pakkun.

Purple Lightning

Developed after losing his Sharingan, a versatile lightning-style technique.

Ninja Ranks & Roles

Kakashi's progression through the ninja ranks:

  • Genin: Graduated at age 5
  • Chunin: Promoted at age 6
  • Jonin: ANBU Captain
  • Leader: Team 7 Sensei
  • Hokage: Sixth Hokage

Key Relationships

  • Obito Uchiha - Teammate who gifted him Sharingan
  • Rin Nohara - Teammate and unrequited love
  • Minato Namikaze - Sensei and Fourth Hokage
  • Might Guy - Eternal Rival and best friend
  • Team 7 - Students he mentored

Notable Facts

Kakashi's famous catchphrase: "Sorry I'm late. I was lost on the path of life."

He permanently wears a mask, with his face remaining one of the great mysteries of the series until later revelations.
