Character Overview

Lelouch Lamperouge, born Lelouch vi Britannia, is the brilliant and complex protagonist of the anime series "Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion."

He is an exiled prince of the Holy Britannian Empire who becomes the revolutionary leader known as Zero, using his genius intellect and supernatural power to challenge the world's greatest superpower.

Multiple Identities

Lelouch Lamperouge

The seemingly ordinary high school student at Ashford Academy. He maintains this facade while secretly plotting his revolution.

Zero

The masked revolutionary leader of the Black Knights. This identity becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed Japanese people.

🎭
Lelouch vi Britannia

The exiled 17th prince of the Holy Britannian Empire, son of Emperor Charles zi Britannia.

The Demon Emperor

His final identity, where he takes control of Britannia to become the world's greatest tyrant, uniting humanity against him.

Key Relationships

C.C.

The immortal witch who grants him the Power of Geass. Their relationship evolves from a simple contract to a deep, complex bond built on mutual understanding and trust.

Suzaku Kururugi

His childhood friend and ultimate rival. Their opposing ideologies and methods create one of anime's most compelling rivalries, with Suzaku believing in changing the system from within while Lelouch seeks to destroy it.

Nunnally vi Britannia

His younger sister and primary motivation. Everything Lelouch does is ultimately to create a gentle world where Nunnally can live happily.

Kallen Stadtfeld

One of his most loyal soldiers in the Black Knights and his most skilled Knightmare pilot. She struggles with her loyalty to Zero versus her growing distrust of Lelouch.

Famous Strategies & Gambits

The Narita Mountain Battle

Used the terrain and psychological warfare to defeat a much larger Britannian force, establishing Zero's reputation as a brilliant commander.

The Black Rebellion

Orchestrated a massive uprising that nearly captured the Britannian capital, demonstrating his ability to coordinate complex, large-scale operations.

The Zero Requiem

His ultimate plan: become the world's greatest tyrant and then have himself assassinated, uniting humanity through shared hatred and creating lasting peace.

The Shanghai Strategy

Outmaneuvered both the Chinese Federation and Britannian forces simultaneously, expanding his influence internationally.

"The only ones who should kill are those who are prepared to be killed."

Why He is Iconic

The Anti-Hero Protagonist

Lelouch redefined what a protagonist could be - willing to commit atrocities for a greater good, making difficult moral choices that challenge the audience.

Intellectual Power Fantasy

His victories come through brilliant strategy and manipulation rather than physical strength, making him unique among anime protagonists.

Complex Morality

He constantly grapples with the consequences of his actions, creating a deeply human character despite his god-like planning abilities.

The Perfect Tragedy

His story follows classical tragic structure - a great man with a fatal flaw, whose greatest strengths lead to his ultimate downfall.

"I destroy the world... and create it anew."

In short, Lelouch vi Britannia is the brilliant, manipulative, and tragic revolutionary who uses his genius intellect and supernatural power to challenge an empire, ultimately sacrificing everything to create a better world.
