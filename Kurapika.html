About Kurapika

Kurapika is a main character in Yoshihiro Togashi's Hunter x Hunter series. He is the sole survivor of the Kurta Clan, whose members were massacred by the Phantom Troupe for their unique scarlet eyes that turn bright red when experiencing strong emotions.

"I don't care if this is a sin. No matter what happens, I will not regret this."

Driven by vengeance and the desire to reclaim his clan's stolen eyes, Kurapika becomes a Hunter and develops a specialized Nen ability specifically designed to combat the Phantom Troupe.

Personality & Character

Kurapika is typically calm, intelligent, and analytical, but becomes consumed by rage when confronting those responsible for his clan's destruction. He struggles between his desire for revenge and his moral principles.

Intelligent Driven Analytical Vengeful Loyal Tragic

Despite his thirst for vengeance, Kurapika maintains strong friendships with Gon, Killua, and Leorio, showing he hasn't completely lost his humanity to his quest for justice.

Nen Abilities & Powers

As a Nen user, Kurapika specializes in Conjuration and Manipulation, creating his signature chains with specific conditions and limitations that make them incredibly powerful.

Scarlet Eyes

When his eyes turn scarlet, he becomes a Specialist and masters all Nen categories at 100% efficiency.

Emperor Time

His Specialist ability that activates with scarlet eyes, granting him mastery over all Nen types.

Chain Jail

His most powerful chain that can only be used against Phantom Troupe members, enforced by a life-forfeiting vow.

Steal Chain

Later developed ability that allows him to steal others' Nen abilities under specific conditions.

Hunter Career

Kurapika became a Blacklist Hunter, specializing in hunting criminals. He later works as a bodyguard for the Nostrade family to gain resources for his mission.

The Vow

Kurapika's Chain Jail carries a deadly restriction: if he uses it on anyone other than a Phantom Troupe member, he will die instantly.

Current Mission

Kurapika is currently focused on retrieving the remaining Scarlet Eyes of his clan from collectors and the Phantom Troupe.
