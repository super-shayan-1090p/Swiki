YUUJI ITADORI

The Vessel of Sukuna

About Yuuji Itadori

Yuuji Itadori is the main protagonist of the manga and anime series Jujutsu Kaisen, created by Gege Akutami. He was an ordinary high school student until he encountered a cursed object and became the vessel for Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses.

"I don't know how I'll feel when I'm dead, but I don't want to regret the way I lived."

After swallowing one of Sukuna's fingers to save his friends, Yuuji becomes a jujutsu sorcerer to locate and consume all of Sukuna's fingers, ultimately allowing for Sukuna's complete eradication upon his own death.

Personality & Character Traits

Yuuji is characterized by his immense physical strength, kind heart, and strong sense of justice. Despite his circumstances, he maintains an optimistic outlook and deep care for others.

Compassionate Determined Physically Gifted Self-Sacrificing Optimistic Strong-Willed

Yuuji believes that helping people is the right thing to do and strives to ensure people have "proper deaths," not meaningless ones caused by curses.

Jujutsu Abilities

As a jujutsu sorcerer, Yuuji possesses exceptional physical abilities and learns to control cursed energy. As Sukuna's vessel, he has access to incredibly powerful techniques.

Superhuman Physicality

Enhanced strength, speed, and durability even without cursed energy.

Divergent Fist

His signature technique where cursed energy impact is delayed after physical contact.

Black Flash

A spatial distortion that occurs when cursed energy is applied within 0.000001 seconds of a physical hit.

Sukuna's Techniques

When Sukuna takes control, he can use powerful techniques like Cleave and Dismantle.

Cursed Energy Levels

Yuuji's progression in cursed energy control:

  • Initial: Raw physical power
  • Training: Basic cursed energy control
  • Divergent Fist: Unique application
  • Black Flash: Master-level technique

Key Relationships

  • Megumi Fushiguro - Fellow jujutsu sorcerer and close friend
  • Nobara Kugisaki - Teammate and friend
  • Satoru Gojo - Mentor and teacher
  • Ryomen Sukuna - The curse inhabiting his body

Mission

Yuuji's goal is to collect and consume all 20 of Sukuna's fingers and then be executed, taking the King of Curses with him to protect humanity from curses.
