The Scientific Genius of the Stone World

About Senku Ishigami

Senku Ishigami is the main protagonist of Riichiro Inagaki's Dr. Stone series. He was a high school science prodigy when a mysterious green light petrified all of humanity for 3,700 years. Senku managed to maintain his consciousness throughout the petrification and eventually broke free, dedicating himself to rebuilding civilization through science.

"Get excited! This is the birth of a new scientific civilization!"

With his catchphrase "Get excited!" and his encyclopedic knowledge of science, Senku leads the effort to revive humanity and advance technology from the Stone Age to the modern era, facing numerous challenges including the antagonistic Tsukasa Empire.

Personality & Character

Senku is a brilliant, pragmatic scientist who approaches every problem with logic and creativity. Despite his scientific detachment, he genuinely cares about people and believes science should be used to benefit humanity.

Genius Intellect Pragmatic Charismatic Leader Resourceful Determined Surprisingly Caring

Senku's greatest strength is his ability to apply scientific principles to real-world problems and inspire others to join his mission. He values human life and sees science as the ultimate tool for progress and protection.

Scientific Abilities

Senku possesses an incredible range of scientific knowledge and practical skills that allow him to recreate modern technology from scratch.

Chemistry Mastery

Expert knowledge of chemical reactions, compounds, and industrial processes. Can create everything from medicine to explosives using primitive materials.

Physics & Engineering

Deep understanding of mechanical principles, electricity, and structural engineering. Designs and builds complex machines from basic components.

Biology & Medicine

Comprehensive knowledge of human anatomy, diseases, and medical treatments. Creates antibiotics and other medicines to treat illnesses.

Mathematics & Calculation

Exceptional mathematical skills for precise measurements, calculations, and scientific predictions. Can perform complex calculations mentally.

Resource Management

Expert at identifying, collecting, and utilizing natural resources efficiently. Knows where to find essential minerals and materials.

Teaching & Leadership

Skilled at explaining complex scientific concepts to others and inspiring them to contribute to his scientific revolution.

The Ten Billion Percent Genius

Senku's mind operates with "ten billion percent" certainty when he has enough data. His scientific predictions are almost always accurate, and he can recall complex formulas and processes from memory.

Petrification Survival

Senku survived 3,700 years of petrification by counting seconds in his mind, maintaining his consciousness through sheer willpower and mathematical discipline 🧪.

Scientific Philosophy

Senku believes science belongs to everyone and should be used to improve human life. He rejects the idea of hoarding knowledge and actively teaches others, creating a new generation of scientists.

Legacy & Impact

Through his Kingdom of Science, Senku demonstrates that human progress doesn't require thousands of years - with the right knowledge, civilization can advance from the Stone Age to modern technology in a single lifetime.
