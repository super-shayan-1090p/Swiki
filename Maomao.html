Character Overview

Maomao is the brilliant and curious protagonist of Natsu Hyuuga's light novel series "The Apothecary Diaries" (Kusuriya no Hitorigoto). She is a young woman with exceptional knowledge of medicine and poisons who finds herself working in the imperial palace's rear court.

Her sharp intellect, dry wit, and unquenchable curiosity make her one of the most unique and compelling heroines in historical fiction.

Maomao's driving force is her boundless curiosity about medicine, poisons, and solving mysteries. She cannot resist investigating anything that piques her intellectual interest.

Key Relationships

Jinshi

The beautiful eunuch who recognizes her talents and draws her into palace affairs. Their relationship evolves from professional to something more complex.

Gyokuyou

The kind consort who becomes Maomao's protector and patron in the rear palace, appreciating her medical skills and honest nature.

Her Father

The apothecary who raised her and instilled her love for medicine, though their relationship is complicated by her unknown origins.

Gaoshun

Jinshi's attendant who often serves as the straight man to Maomao's unconventional behavior and sharp observations.

"I'm just a curious apothecary. I can't help but investigate when something interesting comes along."

Notable Cases & Discoveries

The Poisoned Consorts

Her first major case involving mysterious illnesses among the emperor's consorts, which establishes her reputation in the palace.

The Cursed Jewel

Investigating jewels that seem to cause illness, demonstrating her scientific approach to seemingly supernatural phenomena.

Food Poisoning Incidents

Multiple cases involving tainted food and drink where her poison knowledge proves crucial for identifying culprits and preventing deaths.

Medical Treatments

Developing innovative treatments for various ailments using her extensive knowledge of traditional and herbal medicine.

"It's not that I want to get involved. It's that I can't stand not knowing the answer."

Unique Perspective

Scientific Mind in Ancient World

Maomao brings a modern scientific approach to a historical setting, relying on evidence and experimentation rather than superstition.

Class Consciousness

Her background in the pleasure district gives her a unique perspective on class dynamics and palace politics.

Feminine Intelligence

She demonstrates that intelligence and expertise can be powerful tools for a woman in a restrictive society.

Practical Femininity

She redefines feminine strength through competence, intelligence, and practical skills rather than traditional beauty or charm.

"I suppose I'll taste it."

Why Maomao is Iconic

Unconventional Heroine

She breaks from traditional female protagonist molds with her scientific mind, dry humor, and lack of interest in romance.

Intellectual Curiosity

Her driving motivation is pure intellectual curiosity, making her a refreshingly different kind of protagonist.

Historical Accuracy

Her medical knowledge and the historical setting are well-researched, adding depth and authenticity to the story.

Progressive Values

She represents values of scientific reasoning, gender equality, and class mobility within a historical context.

"I'm not a hero. I'm just someone who can't leave a mystery unsolved or a poison unidentified."

In short, Maomao is the brilliantly curious apothecary of The Apothecary Diaries whose sharp intellect, medical expertise, and unquenchable curiosity make her one of the most unique and compelling heroines in historical fiction.
