Wanda Reyes - Fictional Character

Wanda Reyes is best known as a character from the award-winning Netflix series Orange Is the New Black (OITNB).

Character Overview

Source: Netflix series Orange Is the New Black

Portrayed by: Actor Maria Dizzia

Role: Wife of main character Joe Caputo (played by Nick Sandow)

Character Traits & Role

Personality & Character

Wanda is portrayed as a kind, grounded, and supportive partner. She provides a stable and loving home life for Caputo, which contrasts with the chaos of his job as the prison administrator.

Her character represents the "normal" world outside of Litchfield Penitentiary, offering Caputo an escape from the constant drama of the prison.

"Wanda Reyes provides the emotional anchor for Joe Caputo, representing stability and normalcy amidst the chaos of Litchfield Penitentiary."

Character Development

Wanda becomes a central figure in Caputo's personal life, especially in the later seasons of the show. Her relationship with Caputo evolves throughout the series, showing both the challenges and rewards of their partnership.
